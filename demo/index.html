Totem Alpha

You are viewing an extraordinarily early version of the Totem ground control software by Nevada Dynamics. This is the interface that will be used to control all of your drones, from anywhere in the world. Once the drones are connected to our backend system using various hardware options that we provide, they will become accessible on this page. Everything you see here is being rendered in real time using our fast and secure messaging infrastructure, but the data is fabricated for the purposes of demonstration. Please contact mac@nevadadynamics.com with any questions.

MacCallister Higgins

CEO

MacCallister J. S. Higgins collegiate career encompassed Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno. He has worked at the Polymer Microsystems (PlUS) Lab under Dr. Geiger since 2011 developing lab technologies, fabricating equipment, researching microfluidic methodologies, and assisting in several other projects with both masters and undergraduate students. Having worked on projects for the NASA OMEGA System (designed and built an autonomous mesh network of sensors) and in support of various other grants through the PlUS Lab, he was awarded the Undergraduate Research Award to pursue the development of an on-demand, direct-write microfluidic fabrication system. Currently, he is also doing research in the field of Human-Robot Interaction as well as the large-scale control of Unmanned Autonomous Systems for the UNR Robotics group, including failure contingencies and safety systems in the event of a catastrophic motor failure on rotorcraft UAVs. Email MacCallister at mac@nevadadynamics.com.

Erik Edgington

President

Erik P. Edgington studied Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Entrepreneurship at the University of Nevada. His experience encompasses a wide range of topics including project management, 3D modeling and printing, and financial modeling and projections. Erik’s experience in leading both technical and non-technical teams will help guide Nevada Dynamics’ future and bridge the gap between technical development and the business development side of the company. Email Erik at erik@nevadadynamics.com.

Zachary Carlson

CTO

Zachary T. Carlson is a Sparks/Reno native and graduate from the University of Nevada, Reno with a B.S. in the Computer Science and Engineering. The majority of his career has been a combination of research and software engineering, allowing him to provide a diverse perspective on the direction of Nevada Dynamics’ products and services. Zack leads the design and development of the numerous projects surrounding the development team with an ever growing background in design, web, robotics, and artificial intelligence.
